PythonPH who?

The group started back in 2007 when Jun Auza of TechSource blogged about forming a local Python User's Group. JM Maranan stumbled upon the post and created a Google group.

Last year, we held the first ever PyCon Philippines. Today, we hold monthly meet-ups for knowledge sharing and some pizza.
